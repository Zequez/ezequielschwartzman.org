The Edgeworker's
Flowing Fund Gameworld

New values

Having all-the-time access to large quantities of money accumulated can be undesirable as it can be distracting and make your Box harder to manage.

Having a fresh steady flow of money is desirable.

Deciding beforehand a sufficient amount of money and the purpose (where it will flow from you) of the money that will flow into you is desirable.

Everybody being able to see what you do with the money you receive is desirable.

Enabling people to commit inordinates amounts of money to your evolutinary path is desirable.

New distinctions

Some boxes are better equiped at managing large quantities of money than others.

Different Box designs require different dynamics with money for them to work effectively.

It's only possible to own money when it's given.

Purpose

Money has no value. Yet, making agreements with people about how money will flow, does have value; that's a facet Creative Collaboration; it's a way to signal and agree with others at where value is being created.

The purpose of the Edgeworker's Flowing Fund Gameworld is to create the value of committing to each others committments at scale.

Meta: There is also something about transparency and integrity.

Rules of Engagement

Read this Codex before you start.

Look at least another participant Flowing Fund Page.

Choose a measurable time Cycle with a length.

You make an inventory of all the ways you are flowing money into others and all the ways you want to flow money into others, and then you make categories and decide how much money you want to flow into what at each Cycle Turning. You are invited to look at examples from other participants to be inspired.

Your Flowing Fund Guardian

You pick a Guardian for your Flowing Fund, a person who will hold the money and is responsible for transferring you the money you decided at each cycle.

You may negotiate loans from the Flowing Fund with the Guardian, to be paid back automatically by discounting from next cycles.

Your Guardian may negotiate loans with other participants using your Flowing Fund.

You may negotiate loans with other participants Guardians using the Flowing Funds they are guarding.

Credits

If at a Cycle Turning there is not enough money on your Flowing Fund, you issue Credits for the remaining amount.

Issuing Credits

You will need:

  • A piece of paper big enough
  • Something to draw on the paper
  • A timer

Then create it:

  1. Write (Your Full Name)'s Flowing Fund Credit in the paper.
  2. Write the credited amount.
  3. Write your Flowing Fund Page web address.
  4. Make an empty square about 3cm by 3cm
  5. Set the timer to 30 minutes and draw on the square
  6. Take a picture of the credit issued
  7. Add the picture to your Flowing Fund Page on that Cycle

With this the Credit is issued, and you can now give the paper to other people.

Redeeming Credits

You may redeem the Credit by negotiating with the Flowing Fund Guardian.

Credits may be circulated and used as money in themselves until redeemed. Every time a credit changes hand, a signature is added.

Your Flowing Fund Page

You have a Flowing Fund page publicly available online. On this page the following information is made accessible:

  • Your full real name and picture of you
  • The full real name and picture of your Flowing Fund Guardian
  • Your Flowing Fund Time Cycle
  • Your current decision about how much money you receive at each Cycle Turning and how it flows
  • The total amount of money in the Flowing Fund
  • The list of all inputs of money into the Flowing Fund and from whom or where they came from
  • Instructions about how to add money to your Flowing Fund
  • Instructions about how to redeem credits
  • A section for each Cycle Turning:
    • Cycle Turning date
    • Amount received on that Cycle Turning
    • Credits issued on that Turning, with pictures of the credit and wether the Credit has been redeemed
    • Summary of how it when with the money you received on the previous Turning
