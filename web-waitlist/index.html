Here is what I want

I want to keep creating the Web Environment and developing new substrates and digital creative environments for humans.

And then I tell them "Oh person, I would love to do that, I would probably do it wether you give me money or not, but you know, money can come in very handy to buy food and Internet and electricity and stuff like that, you know? Would you like to be on the waitlist?"

I think I'm going insane. I'm feel afraid that no on will see this and I'll starve to death. Hey Internet! Hey! I'm here! Hey! I'm building something here! Would you send people this way please? Thank you!
